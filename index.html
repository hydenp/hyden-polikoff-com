About Me

I’m Hyden and soon to be a computer science major and applied mathematics minor. My passion is problem-solving with software. I love finding and developing efficient and powerful solutions to challenging problems with code. But engineers are general problem solvers. Whether it is a new script, refining a business process, or another medium that lends itself to the situation at hand, being an engineer and thinking through problems is what gets me excited every day.

My love for technology runs deep, but I have not always been involved with it. I grew up in Marin County California and attended a Waldorf school K through 12. I didn’t have my own computer until graduating high school and my pre-college education focused more on arts and music than science and math. But I also believe that these other experiences make me a far better engineer, teammate, and person than I would be without them.

Recently, my professional focus has been working with and understanding cloud technologies and how best to put the tools available to use. In the future, I look forward to continuing to develop this expertise while also hoping to broaden my experience across the entire stack—front to back.

Beyond professional and academic, I’m an avid basketball player and fan, as well as a snowboarder, mountain biker, and gym goer. Balancing my professional life with things that bring me joy and peace of mind in an entirely different way is something I deeply value.
